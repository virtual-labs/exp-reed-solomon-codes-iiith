### Textbooks

**1. Error Correction Coding: Mathematical Methods and Algorithms**<br/>
BY: Todd K. Moon - John Wiley & Sons, 2020<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of Reed-Solomon codes, finite field arithmetic, encoding algorithms, and decoding techniques including the Berlekamp-Massey algorithm._

**2. Error Control Coding**<br/>
BY: S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004<br/>
_Detailed treatment of Reed-Solomon codes, algebraic decoding methods, and implementation aspects._

**3. The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: F. MacWilliams and N. Sloane - North-Holland Publishing Company, 1977<br/>
_Classic reference on algebraic coding theory with comprehensive analysis of Reed-Solomon codes and MDS codes._

**4. Algebraic Codes for Data Transmission**<br/>
BY: Richard E. Blahut - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_In-depth coverage of Reed-Solomon codes, polynomial representations, and practical applications in data storage and communication._

**5. Reed-Solomon Codes and Their Applications**<br/>
BY: Stephen B. Wicker and Vijay K. Bhargava - IEEE Press, 1994<br/>
_Specialized text focusing on Reed-Solomon code theory, implementation, and real-world applications._

**6. Introduction to Finite Fields and Their Applications**<br/>
BY: R. Lidl and H. Niederreiter - Cambridge University Press, 1986<br/>
_Essential finite field theory required for understanding Reed-Solomon codes and their algebraic structure._
